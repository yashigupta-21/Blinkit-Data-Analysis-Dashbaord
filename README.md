# Blinkit-Data-Analysis-Dashbaord
This project showcases a Sales &amp; Outlet Performance Dashboard for Blinkit (India’s last-minute delivery app), built using Power BI.  It provides deep insights into sales trends, outlet performance, product categories, and customer preferences, helping businesses optimize operations and maximize revenue.

Blinkit Sales & Outlet Performance Summary

This dashboard provides a snapshot of sales performance, product distribution, and outlet efficiency.

Total sales stand at 1.20M with an average sale of 141 across 8,523 items and an average rating of 4, indicating stable customer satisfaction.

Sales trends show steady growth over the years, with a peak followed by slight stabilization. Larger outlet sizes contribute the most to revenue, while Tier 3 locations outperform other regions in total sales.

Product-wise analysis highlights that a few categories drive the majority of revenue, while fat content distribution shows higher contribution from regular products.

Overall, the dashboard helps identify key revenue drivers, outlet performance differences, and product-level opportunities for improving sales and optimization.
